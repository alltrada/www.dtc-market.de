About Us

DTC Market is the trading brand of Drechsler Trading & Commerce, a Germany-based company focused on structured wholesale purchasing and B2B distribution of branded consumer goods.

Our core activity is the professional sourcing of products through established manufacturers and distributors, with a strong emphasis on reliability, transparency, and long-term cooperation.

Products are distributed via established online sales channels, primarily within the German market, in accordance with applicable marketplace and brand requirements.

Our vision is to build stable and scalable supply relationships based on trust, clear processes, and consistent purchasing standards.
