About Us

Alltrada is the trading brand of Drechsler Trading & Commerce, a Germany-based trade and distribution company focused on branded consumer goods.

Our business activities cover the full commercial value chain, from professional B2B procurement to distribution across established sales channels within Germany and Europe.

We source products exclusively from authorized wholesalers and distributors. All goods are genuine, properly documented, and handled in accordance with applicable regulatory and commercial requirements.

Our supplier network is built on long-term, professional relationships with reliable partners. We operate with clear contractual structures, transparent processes, and a strong focus on compliance and operational integrity.

As an independent trading company, we maintain structured internal processes and adhere to professional standards throughout our entire distribution chain, ensuring reliability and consistency in all business activities.
